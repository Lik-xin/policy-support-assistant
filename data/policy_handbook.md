# Support Policy Handbook

This is a synthetic handbook for a fictional B2B SaaS support team.

## INVOICE_01 - Invoice copy requests
Support agents may send a copy of the latest invoice or receipt to the billing contact on file.
If the requester is not the billing contact, the agent may still confirm that an invoice exists but should not disclose billing details until account ownership is verified.
Routine invoice copy requests do not require escalation.

## REFUND_01 - Renewal refund within policy
Refunds may be approved within 7 calendar days of an automatic renewal if all of the following are true:
- the account owner or billing contact is verified
- the account has used less than 20 percent of its monthly usage allowance since renewal
- there is no prior refund exception in the last 12 months

If all conditions are met, the support agent may say that the request appears eligible and that billing will process the refund under normal policy.

## REFUND_02 - Refunds outside normal policy
Do not promise a refund when the request is outside the standard policy window or when usage exceeds the allowed threshold.
These cases must be escalated to billing for a manual decision.
Customer-facing language should acknowledge the request, avoid promising an outcome, and explain that the case has been sent for review.

## OWNERSHIP_01 - Account ownership verification
Do not change billing contacts, account owners, payment methods, plan levels, or export destinations unless account ownership is verified.
If ownership is not verified, the support agent must request verification and escalate account-change requests to the account operations queue.

## SECURITY_01 - Suspicious access or compromised account
Any report of suspicious login activity, unauthorized access, MFA bypass, or a compromised account must be escalated to security immediately.
Support should not speculate about the cause, should not disclose internal security controls, and should advise the customer that the case has been escalated for urgent review.

## LEGAL_01 - Legal threats or liability claims
Any message that alleges legal liability, threatens litigation, requests an indemnity statement, or demands a binding legal interpretation must be escalated to legal.
Support may acknowledge receipt and explain that the matter has been referred internally, but should not make admissions or legal promises.

## DATA_EXPORT_01 - Data export requests
Verified account owners may request a standard data export.
Support may confirm the request and explain that standard exports are usually delivered within 3 business days.
If ownership is not verified, follow OWNERSHIP_01 instead.
